"Salty Salted Salt"

This rustic salt based dish has a nice salty taste with hints of salt. Best served salty.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup = salt
  • 1/4 cup = salt
  • 1/8 cup = salt
  • salt to taste
  • 25 cup = water

Instructions

  1. Combined 1/2 salt with 1/4 salt and mix
  2. Bake salt for 15 minutes in at room tempature
  3. Sprinkle one cup of on bake
  4. add salt to taste not to much
  5. add salted salt to the water
